Why Little Star Deaf and Dumb School is the best preschool program for your child?

  1. The Only Indian Early Learning Program that delivers 575+ Unique Multidisciplinary experiences for every child.
  2. Most-awarded curriculum inspired by Waldorf, John Dewey, Multiple Intelligence & Playway methodologies.
  3. environment that promotes the deepest level of love & enthusiasm for lifelong learning. Scaffolded learning with "I do, We do, You do" approach that conforms to European safety standards.
  4. Emphasis on 16 key skills for physical, cognitive, social, emotional, language & holistic development.

What role our teachers play in the development of your child?

  1. Teach basic skills such as social skills, personal hygiene, number & letter recognition, shapes, and colours.
  2. Help children explore and try things out by using imaginative play and learning activities
  3. Establish rules and procedures that enforce good behaviour and order in the classroom
  4. Read books and stories to the class
  5. Promote mental, social, and physical development by organizing and leading activities such as arts and crafts, field trips, games, and music
